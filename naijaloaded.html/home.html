Wizkid ft Asake - Jogodo

Posted by Jibade on february 2, 2026 11:07pm

"Jogodo"by Wizkid ft Asake is a dazzling Afrobeats anthem released in January 2026 as the lead single from their joint EP titled REAL. Produced by Magicsticks, the song features catchy Yoruba and Pidgin lyrics about celebration,nightlife and uninhibited fun, with Wizkid's smooth melodies and Asake'senergetic flair blending into a vibrant, chart topping groove.
